About Meanwhile in SL

Sri Lankan creativity on the global digital map through premium, cinematic storytelling.

Welcome to Our Digital Destination

Welcome to Meanwhile in SL – your ultimate digital destination for high-quality, engaging, and cinematic Sri Lankan content. From deep dives into local culture to creative storytelling and visual entertainment, we are a team of passionate media professionals, creators, and artists dedicated to putting Sri Lankan creativity on the global digital map.

What started as a shared vision among creators has grown into a thriving community of 9,900+ subscribers (and counting!). We don't just make videos; we capture moments, tell stories, and bring a signature cinematic touch to everything happening meanwhile in Sri Lanka.

THE JOURNEY

Our Journey & History

Rooted in the cultural capital of Kandy, pushing the limits of digital video production.

Meanwhile in SL was founded by a collective of local digital media and video production professionals who saw an opportunity to elevate high-fidelity, premium digital content tailored for a modern audience. Rooted in the cultural capital of Kandy, our journey began with a simple mission: to push the boundaries of local digital media through top-tier visuals, sharp editing, and authentic, engaging storytelling.

Over the years, our team has expanded from executing regional commercial video campaigns and digital branding to producing original, viral content. Along the way, we’ve hit incredible milestones, collaborated with major brands, and established a reputation for pushing creative limits. Today, we stand proud as a premier multi-talented digital media collective, constantly innovating and delivering the high-quality, cinematic content our audience loves.
