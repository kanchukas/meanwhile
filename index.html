Welcome to Meanwhile in SL

We are your ultimate digital destination for high-quality, engaging, and cinematic Sri Lankan content. From deep dives into local culture to creative storytelling and visual entertainment, we are a team of passionate media professionals, creators, and artists dedicated to putting Sri Lankan creativity on the global digital map.

What started as a shared vision among creators has grown into a thriving community of 9,900+ subscribers (and counting!). We don't just make videos; we capture moments, tell stories, and bring a signature cinematic touch to everything happening meanwhile in Sri Lanka.
